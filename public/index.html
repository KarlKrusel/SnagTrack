Setup Guide
Read me first — one-time setup
Most free downloads are locked behind a follow / like gate (usually on Hypeddit). SnagTrack completes those gates automatically using your own SoundCloud / Spotify account, so you have to sign in once. The whole setup takes about 2 minutes — do it before your first download.
1
Enter your details
Open Settings → Identity and fill in your email and name. These auto-fill email-only download gates.
2
Choose your download folder
In Settings → Download Location, click Browse… and pick where your tracks should be saved.
3
Sign in to your accounts most important
This is the "cookies" step — it saves your logins so the gates can be completed as you.
  1. Go to Settings → Login Session and click Open Login Browser. A normal Chrome window opens on SnagTrack's own profile.
  2. In that window, sign in to SoundCloud (required for most gates), and Spotify + Hypeddit if you want those too.
  3. Close that Chrome window when you're done logging in.
  4. Back in SnagTrack, click Save Session. Your logins are now remembered between runs.
4
Pick your download mode
In Settings → Download Strategy, leave it on Hybrid (recommended): email gates stay instant and browserless, while social gates quietly reuse your saved login in the background.
5
Start downloading
Go to the Downloads tab, paste SoundCloud or Hypeddit links (one per line — or a whole chart/playlist), and hit Start Downloads. Tracks land in your folder with cover art embedded.
Troubleshooting
  • "Close the login browser before starting" — the login Chrome window from step 3 is still open. Close it, then download.
  • Downloads suddenly stop working — your SoundCloud/Spotify session likely expired. Just redo step 3 (Open Login Browser → sign in → Save Session).
  • No cover art on a track — only mp3 and wav get embedded art, and only when the file has none already. Toggle it in Settings → Cover Art.
  • A SoundCloud link finds nothing — that track has no Hypeddit free-download gate (some artists use other services). SnagTrack will say so in the Logs.
  • The download browser runs hidden in the background — you don't need to watch it.
